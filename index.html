Marketing Survey Report

Factors Affecting the Purchase of a Product

Total Responses: 54 | FYBSCIT Semester 2

Introduction

The purpose of this survey was to analyze the major factors influencing consumer purchase decisions in today's competitive market. A structured Google Form questionnaire was designed and distributed among respondents to collect quantitative data regarding their buying behavior.

The survey focused on important variables such as age group, occupation, price sensitivity, brand reputation, advertisement influence, and product quality. A total of 54 responses were collected, with the majority belonging to the 18–25 age group, representing young and digitally active consumers.

Analysis of Survey Findings

  • Young Consumers Dominate: The majority of respondents fall within the 18–25 age group, indicating that purchasing trends are strongly influenced by younger, digitally active individuals.
  • Students as Key Buyers: Most respondents are students, suggesting that affordability and value for money play a significant role in decision-making.
  • Price Sensitivity: A large percentage of participants consider price as either very important or important, highlighting the impact of competitive pricing strategies.
  • Digital Advertisement Impact: Social media and influencer marketing show stronger influence compared to traditional TV advertising.
  • Multi-Factor Decision Process: Consumers do not rely on a single factor; instead, they evaluate price, quality, brand reputation, and reviews collectively before finalizing a purchase.
